Public Capabilities

What this hardware stack is built to support.

  • Mobility in rough layouts The Go2W base is prepared for ramps, gravel, rails, and cluttered indoor rescue-style terrain.
  • Inspection and touch tasks The mounted arm extends the robot toward practical competition tasks when the mobile base is stable under load.
  • Field observation and operator support LiDAR, cameras, onboard compute, and teleoperation tools help the team monitor the robot and keep runs safe.
